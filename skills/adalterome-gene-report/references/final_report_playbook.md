# Final Report Playbook

## Executive Claim

Summarize what AD-Alterome supports for the gene in one restrained paragraph. Include event count, PMID count, top terms, and top hypotheses when available.

## Evidence Table

Keep a compact curated evidence table with PMID, term, evidence type, candidate mechanism strata, and curation-layer sentence informativeness. Do not show `EvidenceScore`.

## Mechanism Synthesis

Group evidence by biological process or phenotype. Use cautious language:

- "AD-Alterome evidence links..."
- "Selected sentences suggest..."
- "The database interpretation associates..."

Avoid unsupported phrasing:

- "proves"
- "is the cause of"
- "demonstrates causality"

## Research Gaps

Prioritize gaps by:

1. repeated terms lacking functional validation
2. generic wording despite high sentence informativeness
3. hypotheses supported by few PMIDs
4. missing article metadata
