# AD-Alterome Knowledge Synthesis Contract

This contract defines the output expected from
`adalterome-knowledge-synthesis`.

## Purpose

The output is a researcher-facing knowledge organization packet for expert
evaluation. It is not a final mechanism claim, manuscript result paragraph, or
substitute for domain expert review.

## Required Files

- `knowledge_packet.md`: main review packet.
- `evidence_map.md`: compact landscape and topology view.
- `expert_review_sheet.tsv`: reviewer scoring worksheet.
- `evaluation_record.json`: evaluation design and rubric metadata.
- `provenance_manifest.json`: query, source URL, curation, and cache metadata.
- `data/cache_manifest.json`: task-local raw API cache manifest.

## Required `knowledge_packet.md` Sections

1. `# AD-Alterome Knowledge Synthesis Packet`
2. `## Query Scope and User Intent`
3. `## Analytical Pattern`
4. `## Event Schema Used`
5. `## Evidence Landscape and Coverage`
6. `## Organized Evidence Groups`
7. `## Gene-Alteration-Phenotype/Hypothesis Map`
8. `## Long-Tail Evidence Candidates`
9. `## AI-Organized Synthesis for Expert Review`
10. `## Expert Review Worksheet`
11. `## Original Evidence Traces`
12. `## Limitations and Non-Claims`
13. `## Source Payloads`

## Expert Review Dimensions

Use 1-5 scores with `cannot_judge` allowed:

- traceability
- accuracy
- breadth
- depth
- organization usefulness
- long-tail usefulness
- hallucination or overclaim risk
- inspiration
- review efficiency
- overall usefulness

## Evidence Rules

- Preserve exact original sentences.
- Preserve PMID and PubMed URL when available.
- Do not display or interpret raw `EvidenceScore`.
- Treat AI organization scores as triage signals only.
- Keep `Not applicable to any AD hypothesis` out of named-hypothesis support
  counts, but do not discard those rows solely for mechanism review.
- Report curation endpoint failure instead of falling back to capped event
  samples.

## Publication Framing

Use this wording:

- "AI-organized evidence groups"
- "knowledge packet for expert review"
- "candidate organization"
- "evidence topology"
- "expert evaluation object"

Avoid this wording:

- "AI-discovered mechanism"
- "expert conclusion generated by AI"
- "paper-ready biological claim"
- "causal network inferred by the skill"
